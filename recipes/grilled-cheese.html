Grilled Cheese

Description

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich.

Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of wite bread
  • 3 tablespoon butter, divided
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Equipments

  • Frying pan or any equivalent

Directions

  • Pre-Cooking
    1. Preheat a nonstic skillet over medium heat.
    2. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
    3. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
    4. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  • Cooking Proper
    1. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
    2. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
  • Enjoy!
